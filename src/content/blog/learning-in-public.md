Learning in public turns private notes into small bridges. A rough idea becomes
clearer when you explain it, and clearer still when someone asks a better
question than the one you started with.

I do not treat every post as a final answer. Some posts are field notes, some
are durable essays, and some are simply a record of how my thinking changed.

That habit is useful because software work is never only about tools. It is also
about judgment, taste, communication, and the courage to revise.
