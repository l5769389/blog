Astro content collections are a good default for a personal blog because they
give structure without making publishing feel heavy.

Frontmatter becomes typed data, Markdown remains readable, and pages can query
posts in one place. That means index pages, tag pages, and RSS feeds can all be
built from the same source later.

The main thing to decide early is the shape of frontmatter. Keep it stable,
small, and focused on what the site actually renders.
